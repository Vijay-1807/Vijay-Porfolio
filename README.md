## Projects

### 1. [EduSeater](https://github.com/Vijay-1807/EduSeater)
A web application for college exam seat allocation and invigilator mapping. It allows admins to generate seating plans, ensure no adjacent students from the same branch, and download CSV files for the seating arrangement. Students can log in and view their seat assignments.

### 2. [Legal Buddy](https://github.com/Vijay-1807/LegalBuddy)
A virtual legal assistant powered by AI to assist lawyers with case references, multilingual support, and simplified legal explanations for the general public. The system also generates affidavits, judgments, and case orders efficiently.

### 3. Portfolio Website: https://vijaybontha.netlify.app/ (https://github.com/Vijay-1807/Vijay-Porfolio)
